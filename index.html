My research and teaching are primarily in **computational sociolinguistics**: I study how linguistic differences like the distinction between _working_ and _workin'_ reflect social processes (**sociolinguistics**), and I do so through computational methods like machine learning. I also work to make linguistics more inclusive and equitable.
